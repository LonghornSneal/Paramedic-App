EKG Rhythm Reference

Use these quick cues when selecting a rhythm. Full reference content is available in the EKG Rhythm Reference detail page.

  • Sinus Tachycardia: Regular rhythm, rate > 100 bpm, upright P before each QRS.
  • Sinus Bradycardia: Regular rhythm, rate < 60 bpm, upright P before each QRS.
  • Atrial Fibrillation: Irregularly irregular, absent P waves, fibrillatory baseline.
  • SVT: Narrow complex tachycardia > 150 bpm, P waves often hidden.
  • Ventricular Tachycardia: Wide complex > 100 bpm, monomorphic or polymorphic QRS.
